### Project Questionnaire

**Q1. What does your application/service do?**
It is an AI-Powered Customer Churn Prediction System that helps companies analyze user behavior and accurately predict which customers are likely to cancel their subscriptions.

**Q2. Who is the target audience?**
Telecommunications companies, internet service providers, and customer retention/marketing teams looking to reduce customer churn.

**Q3. Which countries are the expected buyers of this service?**
Global telecommunications operators, with a primary focus on major markets like India, the United States, and European countries.

**Q4. Who are your competitors?**
Traditional enterprise CRM analytics tools, Salesforce Einstein, HubSpot, and custom internal data science platforms developed by larger telecom firms.

**Q5. What is your advantage?**
Our system offers real-time, highly scalable predictive insights explicitly tailored for the telecom sector, providing actionable retention strategies rather than just standard raw data points.
