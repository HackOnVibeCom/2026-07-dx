### Project Questionnaire

**Q1. What does your application/service do?**
It is an AI-powered analytics and marketing system designed to boost the effective promotion of a newly launched mobile app by identifying early user churn signs, predicting user drop-offs, and triggering automated engagement campaigns to keep new users active.

**Q2. Who is the target audience?**
Mobile app developers, startup founders, and digital marketing teams who have recently launched a new mobile app and want to maximize user retention during their promotional phase.

**Q3. Which countries are the expected buyers of this service?**
Global mobile app publishers and tech hubs, primarily focusing on major application markets like India, the United States, and European regions.

**Q4. Who are your competitors?**
Standard mobile analytics platforms like Mixpanel, Amplitude, Firebase Predictions, and traditional app marketing automation tools.

**Q5. What is your advantage?**
Unlike standard tools that only show past drop-off data, our system uses advanced predictive machine learning models optimized for newly launched applications, directly linking churn prediction to immediate, automated promotional hooks and incentives to bring users back.
